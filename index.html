My Projects

"Dual Axis Solar Tracking System"

Six solar panels are wired in parallel with light-detecting sensors, interfaced with an Arduino programmed to track the sun's direction. Voltage output is monitored throughout the day to optimize solar panel orientation for maximum efficiency.

"IOT based realtime Dashboard Visualization from non programmable machines"

An object-detecting sensor connects to an Arduino, feeding data to a custom web dashboard featuring graphs. Users can track real-time production progress remotely, identifying flaws and analyzing daily output from anywhere.

"CFD Analysis of energy efficiency and comfort in Mess Hall"

A CFD analysis evaluates energy efficiency and comfort levels in a Mess Hall, offering insights into airflow, temperature distribution, and thermal comfort to optimize the space for occupants.
